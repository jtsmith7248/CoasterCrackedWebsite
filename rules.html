Rules for Submitting

Every month, the moderators here at Coaster Cracked! run a vote on different
aspects of amusement park related content. We want to make sure that everyone is on
the same page on how to enter their submissions as well as how to suggest new topics
for the community to vote on and talk about.


  1. All submissions to monthly topics will come in the form of Photos, with a caption about what is in them. See below for recent, current, and upcoming questions.
  2. All Photos must follow a strict "no illicit photos" guideline. If it needs to be tagged NSFW, do NOT submit it, you will be perma-banned. This is but a humble coaster-enthusiest website guys, be practical.
  3. File size must be 1MB or under, and filetype of .JPG or .PNG. If you're submitting from an iPhone, make sure you're using the right filetype.
  4. Please make submissions that are on topic or it will not be included.
  5. Maximum 3 Submissions per Month.
  6. The top 5 submissions will be considered in the vote
  7. Finally, the deadlines work as such. On the first of each month, the next month's topic will be announced, as well as whether it was Moderator (M) or Community Member (C) suggested. You must submit your suggestions by 11:59PM in the month prior. So, on July 1st, August's question was announced. Everyone can continue submissions until July 31st at 11:59PM. Then, voting will commence at 12:00PM August 1st, closing at 11:59PM on August 30th, the second to last day of the month. Winners announced at 12:00PM on the final day of every month.

More than anything, make sure to have fun and be kind!
