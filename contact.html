Get In Touch

This page is designated for anyone who feels the need to reach out to me, Justin Smith, the creator of this website. My team and I want to make sure that this community is properly moderated and inclusive for anyone who wishes to talk about our passion for thrill rides, so hate speech, bullying, and general abusive or incosiderate behavior will not be tolerated. I do not expect this to be a problem, but I am more than happy to step in if things start to spiral.


Outside of that heavy forewarning, please feel free to reach out with any comments you may have on the website and community! Please take active use of the Submission Form, however, if you wish to suggest what should be up for a vote next, and/or you have comments on the pictures or anything else related. Thank you so much; I look forward to hearing from you!
